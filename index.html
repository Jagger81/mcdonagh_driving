About our company

With over 20 years experience in the industry, Fairview Driving School offers affordable personlised driving lessons to esnure that you feel comfortable and confident behind the wheel, guaranteeing that you'll be prepared for your test and pass it with ease.

Based out of the Fairview area of Dublin, our expert instructors offer you the opportunity to fully personlise your driving experience with us and are open to focusing on the areas that you feel you need more practice in - we will also identify the areas where we see evident weaknesses and work together to get you ready for test day.

Our full list of services are listed below, and we always welcome a quick phone call to discuss things in detail and assist you with making the right choice.
